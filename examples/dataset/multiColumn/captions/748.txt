The average retail price for butter in Canada was 5 Canadian dollars per 454 grams in December 2019 . This price has fluctuated between months , yet has gradually increased over the given period , with an average price of 4.99 Canadian dollars in 2019 and 4.61 Canadian dollars in 2015 . The Canadian butter market The volume of butter produced in Canada amounted to around 108.77 million kilograms in 2017 , an increase of over 30 million kilograms over the previous ten years . Canada is also active in the international trade of butter , importing around 16 million kilograms in that year . The volume of butter exported is , however , much lower . In 2017 , just 1.18 million kilograms were exported around the world from Canada . When it comes to retail sales , branded products have a clear dominance within the butter market : since 2011 , just one fifth of butter and margarine retail sales were private label products .
