The U.S. market is dominated by foreign imports , and U.S. manufacturers must compete with these lower cost competitors . Much of the market 's performance is driven by consumer segments that have experienced significant slumps in the last several years.No longer simply hard-sided gray suitcases , the luggage industry must maintain a focus on innovation and design while competing against lower cost foreign manufacturers and a fickle tourism industry .
