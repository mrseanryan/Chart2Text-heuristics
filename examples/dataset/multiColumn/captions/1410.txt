In 2019 , the population of Luxembourg amounted to nearly 614,000 people . Of these 614,000 , nearly 309,000 were male . The female population reached just over 305,000 , meaning women in Luxembourg were the minority that year . Population and age When looking at the average age of the Luxembourgish population , it shows that the largest share of people fell in the age brackets between 25 and 55 years old , with those between 35 and 39 forming the largest age group in the Grand Duchy . Only 766 people were 95 years and older . Home to all Luxembourg is home to a large foreign population . In 2019 , nearly 48 percent of the people who live in the country were citizens of another state . That year , most of the foreign citizens came from other European countries . 724 people from Central America also called the Grand Duchy home , as did roughly 2,700 North Americans . In addition , there were also nearly 185,000 frontier workers employed in Luxembourg .
