During the observation period , rugby 's popularity has dropped among children between 11 and 15 years in England and has increased among children between five and ten years . In 2017/2018 , 14.6 percent of 11 to 15 year olds participated in rugby in the 4 years prior to the survey . Participation peaked in 2011/2012 , when 22.8 percent of the children in that age group played rugby . The Rugby Football Union is the governing body for the rugby union in England . The Rugby Football Union received 37 million British pounds for rugby development investment and almost double , 70.7 million British pounds , in professional rugby investment in 2018 . The largest sources of revenue in the same year were broadcasting and hospitality catering with 41.2 million British pounds in revenue each . The number of people participating in rugby union has been declining . In 2018 , there were 239,700 people who participated in rugby union at least twice a month at any intensity for any duration in England . In 2016 , there were 259,600 playing rugby . In 2018 , the most popular sport was running , followed by fitness with respectively 6.8 and 6.5 million monthly participants .
