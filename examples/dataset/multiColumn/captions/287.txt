This statistic shows the number of medium and heavy commercial vehicles that were registered in selected countries across Europe in 2018 . In France , over 55,400 heavy commercial vehicles were registered in 2018 . Truck registrations in Europe With over 94,400 , the leader of heavy commercial vehicle registrations within Europe was Germany . Not only is the European powerhouse king of the European heavy truck market in terms of registrations , but it is also home to key players in the heavy commercial vehicle manufacturing market , including Daimler 's truck segment , as well as Volkswagen 's MAN and Scania . In the fiscal year of 2018 , Daimler Trucks contributed about 38.3 billion to the manufacturer 's total revenue , while Mercedes-Benz Vans contributed about 13.6 billion .
