This statistic depicts Baxter International 's revenue in 2017 and 2018 , distributed by division . Baxter International Inc. is a health company from the United States , active in the manufacturing of medical equipment and pharmaceuticals ( until 2014 ) . Baxter is headquartered in Deerfield , Illinois . In 2017 , the company 's Renal Care division generated approximately 3.48 billion U.S. dollars of revenue .
