Prince Harry was the most-liked member of the British Royal Family , according to a survey conducted just prior to his wedding to Meghan Markle in 2018 . With 42 percent of respondents in Great Britain saying they liked Harry , he was slightly more popular than his brother Prince William at 30 percent , and his sister-in-law Katie at 27 percent . Prince Charles , Harry 's father was only liked by nine percent of people in Great Britain , although his wife Meghan was liked by 14 percent of people . The popular prince As well as being popular in Great Britain , Prince Harry had a global net favorability rating of 32 percent in 2018 , and in no country did he have a negative rating . Harry 's grandmother , Queen Elizabeth is also been a popular figure and enjoyed a favorability rating of 49 percent in Great Britain in 2018 , second only to Romania where she had a favorability rating of 55 percent . Support for the monarchy in Britain While the monarchy has certainly had its ups and downs , it remains a very popular British institution , with people of all age groups expressing support for its continuation in 2018 . Despite this , there is a clear correlation with age , with 77 percent of over 55s supporting the monarchy , compared with 57 percent of 18 - 24 year old 's .
