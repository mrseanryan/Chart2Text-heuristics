The American Dream The United States is well known the world over for being a diverse country with many different ethnicities . This stems from the high numbers of immigrants to the U.S. throughout the country 's history , with many people coming to America in search of a better life and more opportunities . This search for opportunity became known as the `` American Dream '' , and the country became a `` melting pot '' of different nationalities , cultures , and traditions . Looking to the future Currently , the United States is the third most populous country in the world , and its population is only expected to increase . It is forecast that the U.S. population will show steady growth over the next 40 years , and by 2060 , the population of the U.S. is expected to be over 400 million people .
