The number of knockouts and submissions in the Ultimate Fighting Championship ( UFC ) has kept roughly the same proportions over recent years with 145 knockouts and 80 submissions in 2017 . This correlates with the number of fights taking place which also been close to same in since 2014 . The preferred type of submission move is the rear naked choke . Georges St-Pierre holds 2 records As of June 2019 , Canadian Georges St-Pierre still holds the record in the UFC for most takedowns landed during UFC fights with 90 takedowns as well as most strike landed in UFC fights with a total of 2,591 strikes . U.S. American Donald Cerrone holds the record for most knockdowns landed in UFC fights with 20 successful knockdowns .
