This statistic shows the percentage of employees whose pay and conditions were agreed in negotiations between the employer and a trade union ( collective agreement coverage ) in the United Kingdom from 1996 to 2018 , broken down by sector . In the most recent surveyed time period , 58.9 percent of employees in the public sector had their pay and conditions negotiated for them by a union . In comparison , this figure was 14.7 percent for the private sector . Overall , both sectors see a trend of decrease over this time period .
