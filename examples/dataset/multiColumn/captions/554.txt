This statistic shows the divorce rate in Great Britain from 2000 to 2017 . The divorce rate is defined as 'people divorcing per 1,000 married male/female population aged 16 and over ' _ . After increasing year on year from 2000 to 2004 ( a rise of 1.3 ) , the divorce rate dropped consistently until 2009 . For the region of England and Wales , the divorce rate ended at 4.3 points lower in 2017 , compared to 2000 .
