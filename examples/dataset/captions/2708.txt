Dover was the busiest international port in the United Kingdom in 2018 , recording close to 11.8 million passenger movements on short sea routes at the south-eastern ferry port . As the UK harbor closest to the European mainland , it is unsurprising that Dover has become the country 's principal sea port . This was despite a decline in passenger numbers in the past fifteen years .
