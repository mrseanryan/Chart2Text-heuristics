In the fourth quarter of 2019 , Twitter 's revenue amounted to over 1 billion U.S. dollars , a 22 percent increase compared to the previous quarter . The majority of the social network 's revenues are generated via advertising , followed by data licensing and other revenues . Twitter company information Twitter ranks among the biggest social networks worldwide . In the fourth quarter of 2019 , the company reported 152 million monetizable daily active users . In 2019 , Twitter 's annual revenue came to 3.46 billion U.S. dollars . However , the company only reported positive net income for the first time in 2018 , revealing a struggle to monetize users . Twitter marketing and advertising Social media advertising spending in the United States is constantly growing . In 2018 , the social media ad spend per U.S. internet user amounted to 96.8 U.S. dollars and this figure is projected to sharply increase to over 232 U.S. dollars by 2023 . Along with Instagram , Twitter is a popular marketing channel and some brands and companies have made a name for themselves for being entertaining on Twitter .
