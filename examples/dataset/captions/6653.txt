With a market capitalization of over 38 billion euros , Intesa Sanpaolo was the leading bank in Italy listed on the Milan Stock Exchange as of September 2019 . Intesa Sanpaolo , together with its fierce competitor UniCredit , dominates the banking sector in the country . The two banking groups are not only the leaders in the domestic market , but also ranked among the 20 leading banks in Europe in terms of market capitalization in 2019 . Market capitalization Market capitalization , often used as a measurement of a company 's size , is calculated by multiplying the number of outstanding shares by the current market price of one share . Market capitalization is an important metric for investors as it is simple to calculate and is also a good indicator for risk assessment . Banks are usually among the most valuable companies listed on the stock exchange . For example , Intesa Sanpaolo and UniCredit ranked third and fourth respectively among the companies listed on the Milan Stock Exchange with the highest market capitalization . Intesa Sanpaolo goes global Established in 2007 through a merger between Banca Intesa and Gruppo Sanpaolo IMI , the newly formed bank Intesa Sanpaolo has become the leader in the banking sector in Italy . Over the years , the banking group has also developed a strategic presence abroad . The outcome of this international strategy ? Intesa Sanpaolo counted almost six thousand bank branches and 97 thousand employees worldwide as of 2017 .
