Georges St-Pierre is leading the Ultimate Fighting Championship ( UFC ) in most takedowns landed as of June 2019 , followed by Gleison Tibau with 84 takedowns and Demetrious Johnson with 74 takedowns during UFC fights . At the same point in time Donald Cerrone holds the record for most knockdowns in the UFC with a total of 20 . Georges St-Pierre also holds the record for most strikes landed , with 2,591 landed strikes during UFC fights . 474 UFC fight in 2018 In 2018 , a total of 39 UFC events were hosted around the world featuring 474 fights . The highest live attendance in 2018 was at UFC Fight Night 136 – Hunt vs Oleinik with 22,603 ringside spectators and the highest attendance to date was at UFC 193 – Rousey vs Holm in 2015 with 56,214 attendees . Pay-Per-View In 2017 , the UFC was among the 10 most valuable sports business brands worldwide which is in part thanks to its ' Pay-Per-View ( PPV ) business model . The average number of PPV buys has fluctuated over the years , standing at an average of 447 thousand buys per event in 2018 . The most successful event in terms of PPV buys was UFC 229 – Khabib vs McConor in 2018 with roughly 2.4 million buys .
