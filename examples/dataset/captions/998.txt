The statistic shows a ranking of Major League Soccer teams according to their value . In 2019 , Atlanta United was the most valuable MLS team with a team value of 500 million U.S. dollars . MLS franchise values - additional information Major League Soccer , which played its first season in 1996 , includes 24 teams , of which 21 are located in the U.S. and three in Canada . Even though the league has struggled with attendance in the past , attendance at MLS games has improved steadily since 2008 . During the 2018 season , the average per game attendance at MLS games stood at just under 22 thousand .
