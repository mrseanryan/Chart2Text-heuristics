Movie viewership Movies have long been a staple of the entertainment industry in both the United States and across the globe . Around 19 percent of U.S. adults claim to watch or stream movies every day , while an additional 26 percent state that they watch movies several times per week . Streaming services like Netflix and Hulu have provided consumers with a way to watch films on the go , while theatres continue to provide people with an entire movie-going experience . Despite this added competition from streaming services , annual ticket sales have remained relatively stable for the past 10 years .
