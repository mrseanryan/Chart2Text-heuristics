In 2019 , Mexico produced 351.7 million liters of tequila . In that year , 70 percent of that production was destined to exports . Tequila is made from the agave plant . The two basic categories include ' 100 % agave ' and 'Mixtos ' _ . The first category ' 100 % agave ' includes tequila made from 100 percent blue agave , whereas the category 'Mixtos ' is a blended variety with sugar .
