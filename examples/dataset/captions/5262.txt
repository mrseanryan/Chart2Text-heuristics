Among the websites of women 's magazines in the United Kingdom ( UK ) , as defined by the National Readership Survey , tesco.com was the most visited . Tesco.com , the website of the retailer Tesco with the print magazine titled Tesco - Food Family Living , attracted 3.498 million views monthly from October 2016 to September 2017 . Top selling print editions Between January and December of 2018 , Cosmopolitan was the leading women 's lifestyle and fashion magazine by copies sold , with more than 334 thousand copies making their way off the shelves . The top ten sold more than 2.1 million copies between them . Across the UK , some 373.8 million print sales of magazines took place in 2018 . Shifting consumption patterns Despite more than 370 million magazine sales occurring during 2018 in the UK , the volume of print sales has more than halved since 2011 . The value of print magazine sales in the UK has also suffered in recent years , falling by almost 60 million British pounds between 2017 and 2018 . Although sales have continued to fall for print magazines , it appears that there is still an appetite for the content . As of 2017 , over two thirds of individuals in the UK now consume either news , newspapers or news magazines online .
