The Wimbeldon Championships , held annually over the course of 13 days at the All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club in Wimbledon , London is the oldest tennis tournament worldwide . In 2019 , a total of 500,397 visitors attended the event over the duration of the tournament , an increase from the previous two years . TV reach The 2018 Championships had a total cumulative reach of approximately 26 million on BBC and 29.42 million on ESPN . Event management In 2019 , roughly six thousand employees were hired for Wimbeldon , mostly for catering food and drinks , which made up for half of employment during the tournament , followed by stewards , housekeeping , umpires and transport services . The remainder of staff were made up of ball boys and girls , facility management and other game related tasks .
