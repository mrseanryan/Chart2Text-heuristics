As of June 2019 , out of all Ultimate Fighting Championship ( UFC ) events so far UFC 193 – Rousey vs. Holm on November 14th 2015 in Melbourne , Australia had the highest gate attendance with a total of 56,214 attendees , followed closely by UFC 129 – St. Pierre vs Shields on April 30th 2011 in Toronto Canada with a total attendance of 55,724 . Conor McGregor highest overall direct fight earnings When looking at the highest total earnings among fighters , directly resulting from UFC fights , Conor McGregor , who officially retired on March 26th 2019 from Mixed Martial Arts ( MMA ) , is still at first place with approximately 12 million U.S. dollars . However it is safe to say that this only a small fraction compared to his entire earnings , including Pay-Per-View bonuses , endorsement deals and not to mention his boxing match against Floyd Mayweather , for which alone he made more than 75 million U.S. dollars . 474 UFC fights in 2018 There are plenty of UFC fighters around the world , next to other MMA fighters of different categories . In 2018 , a total of 474 UFC fights took place worldwide , a number which has fluctuated since 2014 , before which it was considerably lower . The highest pay-per-view buy rate among UFC events was at UFC 229 – Khabib Nurmagomedov vs Conor McGregor with a total of 2.4 million buys .
