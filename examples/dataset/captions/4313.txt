In 2019 , the harmonized inflation rate in the Netherlands was 2.7 percent . This was the highest level of inflation reached since 2012 , when the inflation was at 2.8 percent . Of the three Benelux countries , the Netherlands saw the lowest inflation . In 2018 , the harmonized inflation rate was 2.3 percent in Belgium . Inflation in Luxembourg was only slightly lower . HCIP forecast In the next three years , the harmonized inflation rate in the Netherlands was forecast to be between 1.4 and 2.6 percent annually . According to the European Commission forecast , the inflation rate would peak in 2019 , before slightly decreasing again the next years .
