In February 2019 , it was found that 38 percent of adults in the United States aged between 18 and 29 years used Twitter . This age group was the microblogging service 's biggest audience in the United States , followed by a 27 percent usage reach among 30 to 49-year-olds . Twitter users in the United States As of the first quarter of 2019 , Twitter had 68 million monthly active users in the United States . During the same period , the number of monetizable daily active Twitter users in the country amounted to 28 million . As of April 2019 , 56 percent of U.S. Twitter audiences were male and 44 percent were female . According to a November 2018 survey of social media users in the United States , Twitter was the most popular social network for news consumption .
