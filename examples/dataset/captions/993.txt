Beauty videos are big business on YouTube . In 2018 , beauty-related content generated more than 169 billion views on the video platform . Popular types of YouTube beauty content include tutorials and DIY videos , reviews , haul videos and videos produced by beauty vloggers . Beauty content on YouTube/br > Fashion- and beauty-related content such as makeup and skincare tutorials , cosmetics hauls and product recommendations are among the most popular content on YouTube . Campaign films accounted for the biggest share of beauty content video views on YouTube , generating more than a third of views as of August 2018 . Favorites or reviews and tutorials were ranked second and third with 15 and 14 percent of views respectively . YouTube usage in the United States As of 2018 , there were an estimated 192 million YouTube viewers in the United States . According to the Statista Global Consumer survey , 57 percent of users accessed the video platform on a daily basis . Following celebrities and brands were the most important usage reasons .
