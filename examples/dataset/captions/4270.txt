In 2017/2018 , Serie A ranked fourth in match attendance with a total of 9.4 million attendees . Juventus FC had the highest market value among Serie A teams . In 2019 , Juventus was estimated at 782.5 million euros in market value . It was followed by SSC Napoli with 563 million euros and FC Internazionale with 557.4 million euros . During the 2018/2019 football season , Juventus paid the highest average annual salary to its ' clubs players out of all teams in the Serie A with an average of roughly 8.85 million U.S. dollars . The team has been holding the first position as a highest paying club for the past three years . In 2018/2019 , it was followed by Internazionale and Milan which payed its players an average of 4.88 million U.S. dollars and 4.67 million U.S. dollars .
