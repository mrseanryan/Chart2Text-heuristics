The global organic chocolate market was projected to grow in size from 734 million U.S. dollars to 886 million U.S. dollars between 2012 and 2018 . As such , organic chocolate seems to have increased in popularity in recent years . Organic Chocolate Candy A bar of organic chocolate may seem indistinguishable from a bar of regular chocolate ; the biggest difference between the two is that organic chocolate is made from cocoa beans grown without the use of synthetic pesticides or herbicides , as well as with organic sugar and milk . In the United States , organic chocolate confectionary makes up the majority of the organic confectionary market , with retail sales of about 261 million U.S. dollars in 2017 .
