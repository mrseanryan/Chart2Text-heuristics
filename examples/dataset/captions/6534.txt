Over the past 10 years the number of members of the FC Bayern Munich , one of the most prominent football club of the German Bundesliga , the top tier football league of Germany , almost doubled . In 2008 there were roughly 151 thousand members of the club and in 2018 there were 291 thousand . In a survey based on public interest among Germans from 2018 , Bayern Munich lead in as the most popular club and the number of fan club members also came close to doubling in the last 10 years . Highest enterprise value of German clubs The enterprise value of Bayern Munich keeps increasing and stood at approximately 2.5 billion euros in 2018 , the fourth highest in Europe at this point in time . Player salaries also increased , reaching an average annual first-team player salary of roughly 6.35 million in British pounds or 8.36 million U.S. dollars . Revenue and profits 2018 was the first year since 2011 in which profits after tax decreased , from 39.2 million euros to 29.5 million . The gross revenue during this year still increased and stood at 657.4 million euros . The EBITDA came to 136.5 million euros .
