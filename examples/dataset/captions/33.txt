In 2018 , almost 13 percent of U.S. residents aged 30 to 49 years had used an accountancy service within the last 12 months . The number of accountants and auditors has remained at around 1.2 million in recent years . In 2018 , there were 1.26 million people employed in these professions , this was forecasted to increase to 1.4 million by 2022 . Although the sector is expected to flourish in terms of numbers , there is a considerable gender pay gap within the accounting and auditing industry . In 2017 , men earned around 1,389 U.S. dollars per week where as women earned roughly 1,065 U.S. dollars per week for doing the same job .
