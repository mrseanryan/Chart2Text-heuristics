This statistic represents major companies in the global industrial robot market in 2017 , based on revenue . In 2017 , Germany-based KUKA generated some 1.4 billion euros in revenue . Midea of China holds a 74.55 percent voting stake in KUKA . The robotics industry is a subsector of the automation industry . This industry includes a variety of products and services , including sensors and drives , relays , switches , machine vision and control systems , as well as industry software development and services .
