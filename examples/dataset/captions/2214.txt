The statistic compares the total brand value of North American sports leagues in 2013 . All teams of the National Football League had a combined brand value of 9.1 billion U.S. dollars . A brand is defined here as the trademark and associated intellectual property . To calculate brand values the Royalty Relief method was used . This approach assumes the company does n't own their brand and must license it from a theoretical third party . The method determines how much it would cost to do this .
