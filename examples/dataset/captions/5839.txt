As of June 2018 , at the beginning of the 2018 FIFA World Cup , Kylian Mbappé was the the most valuable French football player . His market value reached 120 million euros and became even higher for the 2018 - 2019 season after a successful World Cup for France . Playing as a forward for Paris Saint-Germain ( PSG ) in the French Ligue 1 , Kylian Mbappé was also the club 's most valuable player . Born in 1998 , Mbappé is not only considered one of the best players in the world , his career also shows the importance of money in football in recent years . France : most valuable team of 2018 According to figures from Transfermarkt , France was the national football team with the highest market/transfer value in 2018 . Its market value reached 1.08 billion euros , whereas the Germany squad , winner of the 2014 World Cup , had a value of 884 million euros . Performances , but also age , current club and other clubs ' interest determine the value of a football player in the transfer market . Thus , Antoine Griezmann , second most valuable French player , had a market value of 100 million euros in 2018 . His market value was higher than the ones of Toni Kroos , most valuable German player , and Sergio Busquets , most valuable Spanish player . However , the French team has not always been the most expensive team in the world . In 2016 , Paul Pogba was the most valuable player of the national team . His market value amounted to 70 million euros for 90 million euros in 2018 . In comparison France was listed fourth in the ranking of the 2014 FIFA World Cup most valuable football squads . The explosion of football players values Since 2010 , the total transfer fee spending of the Big-Five league football clubs increased . European football clubs spent more than 5.1 billion euros during the summer transfer window in 2017 , compared to 1.8 billion in 2011 . The value of football teams has exploded since then with the English Premier League being the most expensive league on the transfer market . Football clubs are richer and ready to spend more money on famous players . In 2018 , Juventus Turin spent 117 million euros on Cristiano Ronaldo .
