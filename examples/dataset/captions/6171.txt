This statistic shows the monthly trend in the amount spent on watches and jewelry ( sales value ) in Great Britain from January 2016 to September 2019 , as an index of sales per week . During this period of time , retail sales increased significantly , measuring at 140 index points in September 2019 . The figures are seasonally adjusted estimates , measured using the Retail Sales Index ( RSI ) and published in index form with a reference year of 2016 equal to 100 .
